My Daily Audience

Target Audience

The target audience of Rexburg Temple Inn & Suites are people who love to go to the temple and serve there in the main events like sealings, weddings, receptions and stake sessions. This audiences with their families and friends also love to go around the temple area to ponder, relax and to have a good time with their dear ones.

Two Personas

Persona 1

Young people who enjoy activities outside and in the same time to serve in the Temple. The average age is 20 years old and access the internet on mobile devices to check the website.

Persona 2

Older or mature adults who need to keep informed in every moment and wherever they are because they plan activities in the temple. Their average age is 30 years old and over.

Scenarios

Scenario 1
I have a sealing session with my friends tonight. Do I need extra clothes?
Scenario 2
I am going to attend a baptistery session tomorrow morning. How will the weather be like?
Scenario 3
I have an important meeting this evening by the temple garding or parking lot. Is it going to rain?
Scenario 4
I have to attend a temple wedding next Friday. Is it booked for that day?
Scenario 5
I am planning my next Temple. I need extra information to choose different clothes according to the weather at that time.
