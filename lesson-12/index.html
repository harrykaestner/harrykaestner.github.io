Site Purpose

The purpose of the website is to provide proper accommodations for Temple workers, visitors and their families while staying in Rexburg Temple Inn & Suites. The main goal here is to provide the necessary information about the services available in the hotel to receive well the people coming to serve in the temple or who participates in the main events like sealings, weddings, receptions and stake sessions.
