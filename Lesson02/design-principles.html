Contrast

Pixlr

pixlr.com

This screenshot from Pixlr is an absolutely perfect example using contrast to improve design.

The black background not only strongly contasts the white text contained within it, but it also strongly contrasts the light blue background on the body of the page.

The font choice of the header is bold in contrast to the thinner font used for the paragraph text and the added orange pops as a complementary and therefore contrasting color to the blue.

White Space

SoloLearn

www.sololearn.com

SoloLearn arranges its many icons and text in a way that leaves plenty of breathing room.

Each language tutorial is cushioned inside a card with white space. In between the icon, header, paragraph, and button there is an equal amount of white space; enough to separate the different items of the same card while still suggesting a relationship between them.

There is white space in the form of margins between each card and on the right and left sides of each card.

Heirarchy

Target

www.target.com

The first thing you see on this page is the text displaying a "30% off" discount since it is the largest text on the page.

Next your eyes are drawn to the next largest font that describes what it is you are getting your discount on: "women's clothing..." and then: "3 days only, ends Saturday".

Your eyes then move to the smaller text of the page and the highly contrasting buttons and navigation bar.
